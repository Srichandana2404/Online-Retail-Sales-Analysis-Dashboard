# Online-Retail-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
## Business Insights

### 1. Revenue Overview
The business generated 8.91M in total revenue from 18.5K orders and served approximately 4.3K customers.  
A total of 5M items were sold, indicating strong product demand across multiple categories.

### 2. Sales Trend Over Time
The monthly revenue trend in 2011 shows fluctuations in the first half of the year followed by a strong increase toward the end of the year.  
November recorded the highest revenue, suggesting seasonal demand possibly driven by holiday shopping.

### 3. Orders by Day of the Week
Order activity varies across the week.

- Thursday records the highest number of orders
- Sunday has the lowest number of orders

This indicates that customers tend to place more orders during mid-week.

### 4. Revenue by Country
The majority of revenue comes from the United Kingdom, contributing more than 7M in total sales.  
Other countries such as Netherlands, EIRE, Germany, and France contribute smaller portions of revenue.

This indicates that the business is heavily dependent on the UK market.

### 5. Top Customers
A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of revenue.

- Customer 14646 generated the highest revenue (~0.28M)
- Followed by customers 18102 and 17450

This suggests the presence of high-value repeat customers.

### 6. Top Performing Products
The top revenue-generating products include:

- Paper Craft Little Birdie
- Regency Cakestand
- White Hanging Heart T-Light Holder
- Jumbo Bag Red Retrospot

These products contribute significantly to total revenue and represent high-demand items.

### 7. Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis:

- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products
- Develop loyalty programs for high-value customers
- Expand sales in international markets beyond the UK
- Prepare inventory for peak sales periods toward the end of the year
